The ESNcard

Through the ESNcard you are an official member of the Erasmus Student Network. You get many discounts in many cities and countries in Europe. There are many different (mostly financial) benefits for you from the local, national and international partners of ESN. The card is valid for 12 months from the date of signature and is only valid if you have pasted a photo of yourself in it and have given your real name. Please note that the card you have purchased is only valid for you and may only be used by you. As an overview:

  • international partners on esncard.org
  • national partners on esn-germany.de/partners
  • local partners in Freiburg

In addition to the official discounts, you can get a reduced entrance fee for some events and parties from ESN Freiburg e.V.!
